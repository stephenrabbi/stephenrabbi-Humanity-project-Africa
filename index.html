Humanity Project Africa

Humanity Project Africa is a non-governmental organization that believes that an individual has not started living until he can rise above the narrow confines of his individualistic concerns to the broader concerns of all humanity and to be able to achieve a world of full of love, equity and equal opportunity for all, We must work collectively as a global community to be able to overcome the challenges that we face today. Bringing about a second order social change require the effort of all regardless of age, gender, race, education, sexual preference, language, social status, religion or political views.
our mission is to work collaboratively with local and international organizations, NGOs, Private and public educational institutions, hospitals and primary healthcare centres, faith-based organizations, Government agencies and local community leaders across Africa to identify and develop sustainable projects that emphasize local community development and well-being of the people living in the grassroots with focus on health care, Education, Youth leadership, women entrepreneurship development, support and micro-financing.

Health Care

Communities enlightenment, prevention and treatment

Health Care Services

Through our Research and Documentation, we enlighten people living in local communities on disease prevention and resilience. Our goal is to improve awareness and access to quality Health care services for people living in local communities across Africa and in the IDP camps by 2021 we focused on cancer awareness and treatment, Maternal and child health, Art in health care and Climate change. We apply an integrated approach to improving these health challenges by equipping key actors with the knowledge, attitude and skills necessary for effective and enhanced service to communities and humanity in general. This we do through awareness and enlightenment creation, advocacy and training.
Our objectives is to train, mentor and provide key resources to enable primary health care workers to prescribe and attend to patients in need of quality health care services among the people living in local communities in African by 2021. Secondly we plan to identify, train and support 200 Community Volunteer Workers 70 spiritual leaders and 70 traditional birth attendant to identify and refer Cancer and Maternal healthcare patients to their nearest health facility or quality healthcare service providers. Thirdly to provide support to vulnerable cancer patients for investigation, treatment; chemotherapy and radiotherapy and essential medicines to cancer patients. Fourthly to make use of art as a therapy tool in improving and beautifying primary healthcare centers across Africa by 2021. Lastly providing enlightenment and training to people living in local communities across Africa on how to combat climate change and resilience

Education

Art and STEM in Education,Right and civic responsilities.

Education

Education is one of the most powerful weapons in fighting poverty. However, countries lacking substantial educational infrastructure can face a number of unique problems: rural access, gender inequalities, child labor, and more. These problems required equally unconventional solutions which we at humanity project African are seeking to archive.
We are working to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all, this constitutes the core of a single, renewed education agenda: SDG4-Education 2030. Recognizing the important role of education as a main driver of development, targets for education are also included under several other SDGs; notably on health, growth and employment, sustainable consumption and production, and climate change. Our goal is to improve regular and vocational educational services for students living in local communities across Africa, we focused more on child right to quality education, STEM education for girls, Art in Education and political enlightenment and civic responsibilities for students in higher institutions across Africa. We believe that one book, one pen, one child, and one teacher can change the world.

Youth Leadership

Youth Leadership, development and inclusion

Youth Leadership

ALL over the world, youths are regarded as the future leaders of the society. This is not a misplaced or wistful thinking as this class of energetic citizens possess enormous and multi-dimensional talents, powers and creative abilities which if molded and harnessed in a fit and proper atmosphere, can translate to generational wellbeing in the social, political and economic life of the society. It is against this backdrop that nations, particularly those subscribing to democratic norms and values, evolve deliberate policy measures aimed at prioritizing genuine issues geared towards giving eloquent expression to youth development, self-actualization and repositioning for future leadership.
In Africa, the youths, particularly in the pre -independence era and shortly thereafter, played formidable roles leading to monumental gains in politics, economy, technology, commerce and industry just to mentioned few but the youths of this generation are facing a lot of challenges which has hamper their productivities Recognizing these challenges and the need to engage youth in sustainable development programs, Humanity Project Africa, mobilizes and engages youth across Africa to solve leadership and youth development challenges through community action programs, youth mentorship, career development, civic engagement, leadership training and fellowships.

Empowerment and Suppoort

Women Empowerment and Support program

We spent most of our time promoting opportunity for women and girls because women are necessary contributors to economic development and jobs creation. We believe that a woman multiplies the impact of an investment made in her by extending benefits to the world around her, creating a better life for her family and building a strong community, we must accept that women are better entrepreneurs and give them priority in accessing to financing, as their inclusion in business networks is critical to any country’s entrepreneurial success. Our main purposes is to strengthen women by giving them support, skills, resources, empower and identity as informal workers. Beyond that, we try to work on women’s rights using non-violent methods of demonstrations Our work have spread like wildfire in the last decade. We are becoming the lifeline of marginalized and disadvantaged local communities’ women and young girls, who have suffered abuse, neglect, domestic violence and gender injustices giving them reason to live.
