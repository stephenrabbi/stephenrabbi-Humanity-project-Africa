Our Projects

Our works include: Environmental Sustainability, Poverty Eradication, Quality Health Care and Youth Leadership. Humanity Project Africa primary purpose is to create lasting solutions through continuous projects implementation that is focused on impacting lives at the grassroots

One Love Project

The one love project of Humanity Project Africa focuses on bringing relieve to women and children who are living in local communities across Africa. Our work is targeted at the poor, the homeless, the needy, women who are victims of insurgency and people living in IDP camps across Africa.

Educational Project

Education is one of the most powerful weapons in fighting poverty. However, countries lacking substantial educational infrastructure can face a number of unique problems: rural access, gender inequalities, child labor, and more. These problems required equally unconventional solutions which we at humanity project African are seeking to archive.
We are working to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Health Care Services

Through our Research and Documentation, we enlighten people living in local communities on disease prevention and resilience. Our goal is to improve awareness and access to quality Health care services for people living in local communities across Africa.

Youth Leadership Project

ALL over the world, youths are regarded as the future leaders of the society. This is not a misplaced or wishful thinking as this class of energetic citizens possess enormous and multi-dimensional talents, powers and creative abilities which if molded and harnessed in a fit and proper atmosphere, can translate to generational wellbeing in the social, political and economic life of the society. It is against this backdrop that nations, particularly those subscribing to democratic norms and values, evolve deliberate policy measures aimed at prioritizing genuine issues geared towards giving eloquent expression to youth development, self-actualization and repositioning for future leadership.

Women Empowerment and Support Project

We spent most of our time promoting opportunities for women and girls because women are necessary contributors to economic development and jobs creation. We believe that a woman multiplies the impact of an investment made in her by extending benefits to the world around her, that is why we have considered them a priority in our initiative.
