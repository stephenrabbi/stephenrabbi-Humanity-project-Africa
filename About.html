Our History

Humanity project Africa is a global community of volunteers and sponsors, working together to better the lives of vulnerable women and youths. We started in 2010 as a small women business club called the homeperneur club later empower Africa network, but we later lengthen our tentacles by using innovative method and skills to solve problems for humanity in the feeds of education, youth development, healthcare, women empowerment and micro-financing by working collaboratively with local and international organizations and stakeholders who are interested in our focus area. Our project has directly impacted over 3200 women and children and has indrectly impacted 800 others. We have been working with local community leaders across Africa to identify and develop sustainable projects that emphasize local community development and well-being of the people living in the grassroots especially those that are often left behind. We believe that a tree cannot make a forest, the bigger we are the more our impact will be please JOIN US TODAY.
